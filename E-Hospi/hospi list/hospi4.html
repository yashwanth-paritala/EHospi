FORTIS MALAR,CHENNAI



Fortis Malar Hospital formerly known as Malar Hospital is a multi super-specialty hospital located at Adyar in Chennai, India. Malar hospital was established in 1992. Later in the year 2007 Fortis Healthcare Group acquired the complete stakes in the hospital which later become into Fortis Malar Hospital.
